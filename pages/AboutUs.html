About Us

Welcome to Pumpkin Cinema, Dublin's cinematic haven! Immerse yourself in a world of unparalleled movie magic at our state-of-the-art theaters. From blockbuster hits to indie treasures, Pumpkin Cinema caters to all tastes. Our cozy seating, gourmet snacks, and top-notch service ensure a memorable experience. Follow us on social media for exclusive screenings and events. Pumpkin Cinema isn't just a theater; it's a cultural hub, fostering a love for film in the heart of Dublin. Join us for an extraordinary cinematic journey where every visit is a celebration of storytelling. Welcome to Pumpkin Cinema, where movies come to life!
